🐯 Zoos Victoria membership — should you?

How it works: Zoos Victoria has no Family tier. You buy per-adult memberships at $149/year (~$12.45/month). Kids under 16 from the same household enter free with a member, on any day.
Pay per visit (no membership):
• Werribee Sun (weekend, kids free): 2 adults × $54.50 = $109
• Healesville Tue (weekday): 2 adults × $54.50 + 2 kids (4yo + 9yo) × $28.50 = $166
Total: AU$275
1-Adult Membership ($149): member adult free both visits + 2 kids free both visits (household benefit) + other adult $109 = AU$258
2-Adult Memberships ($298): both adults free + 2 kids free both visits = AU$298 ($23 more than single visits)
Verdict: 1-adult membership saves AU$17 on this trip alone, plus 12 months of free re-entry for the member adult + both kids across Melbourne Zoo, Werribee & Healesville. Buy 1, not 2. Verify current pricing.
